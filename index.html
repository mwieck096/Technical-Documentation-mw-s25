Introduction to Red Dead Redemption 2

Overview

Red Dead Redemption 2 is an open-world western action-adventure game by
Rockstar Games in 2018 (Also known as the game that made me cry). Set in
1899, it follows the story of Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang in the
twilight years of the age of the outlaw. The game features an extensive and
detailed open world. Players interact with the world through a mix of
exploration, combat, and choice-driven dialogue. This documentation serves to breakdown integral gameplay mechanics.

Character Interactions

Arthur Morgan can walk, sprint, jump, crouch, and ride horses. The player has a wide array of controls (combat, horseback, exploration).

  • Greet NPCs (will result in a gain of honor)
  • Antagonize or rob (will result in a loss of honor)
  • Loot containers (or harvest parts from animals)
  • Mount/Dismount horse (as well as pet or brush the horse)
  • Use tools from the wheel (such as the lasso or weapons)

Weapons and Crafting

The game includes revolvers, rifles, bows, and melee weapons (such as the lasso). Weapons must be maintained or they will degrade and lose effectiveness.
Crafting is done at campfires using gathered resources. Players can craft animal
bait, horse tonics, special ammunition, and more.

Honor and NPCs

The honor system reflects moral choices made throughout the game. Higher honor unlocks positive outcomes (lower store prices and trust from NPCs); lower honor leads to fear and hostility (higher prices and greater consequences for crime). Environment

RDR2 includes weather systems, wildlife, and time-based events. Shops close at night and certain wildlife appear only during specific times. Weather affects NPC schedules and environmental interactions. Game lighting and sound design shift based on the time of day.
